# Mega Hiring Workflow

Complete hiring package generator. Takes a job description and core competencies and produces everything needed to hire for an executive role in your organization.

## What This Skill Generates

When triggered with a role and competencies, this skill creates:

1. **4 Interview Guides** (separate documents, one per stage):
   - Recruiter Screener (20-30 min, initial qualification)
   - Hiring Manager Interview (60-75 min, depth assessment)
   - Panel Debrief (45-60 min, problem-solving & analysis)
   - CEO One-on-One (20-30 min, cultural fit & vision alignment)
   - All with: competency-based questions, indented probes, clean format (no "listen for" sections)

2. **Performance Task** (standalone 3-4 hour assessment):
   - Real-world scenario relevant to the role
   - Clear instructions and deliverables
   - Designed to assess multiple competencies in action

3. **Performance Task Rubric**:
   - 5 dimensions of evaluation
   - 4 proficiency levels (Foundation/Intermediate/Advanced/Expert)
   - Scoring guidance

4. **Listening Tour Materials**:
   - Agenda for stakeholder sessions (structured conversation guide)
   - Audience-facing guide (welcoming, non-technical overview)
   - Explains the role and invites input

5. **Core Competencies Rubric**:
   - All 5 competencies defined
   - 4 proficiency levels for each
   - Clear behavioral examples at each level

6. **Google Forms Generator Structure**:
   - Interview feedback form template for each stage
   - All fields pre-specified: interviewer name, candidate name, date, competencies, comments, recommendation scale
   - Ready to copy into Google Forms

All documents follow your organization's formatting standards:
- Avenir font
- 0.5-inch margins
- Probes indented
- Clean structure with NO "listen for" / "red flags" / "green flags"

To use this skill effectively, you need to provide:

2. **Job Description** — Full JD with responsibilities and qualifications
3. **Core Competencies** — List of 5 competencies with:
   - Name of competency
   - Definition/description
   - What a strong candidate looks like (behavioral examples)
